St Aloysious Girls Secondary School was initiated by the Ursuline Fransiscan Sisters on 16/01/2012.It begun with very humble beginnings of both students and infrastructures but now with God's grace infrastructures are sufficient.This school is located at Kibaha in a village called Visiga-Zegereni.

Educational Level Offered

Also the school offers only the Ordinary Level of Education(O-Level) and it admits only girls.The type that is offered is Mathematics,Science,Bsiness,Social Studies and Religion.
There about 14 teaching and 8 non teaching staff together with some members from St. Claire convent.
